# NarrativeOS Roadmap

A staged plan from a single-novel continuity tool to a project-agnostic engine for prose research and elite generation.

---

## Phase 12 — Chaos & Recovery (Completed)

**Goal:** Prove NarrativeOS can survive failures without data loss.
- **Crash Injection Harness**: Verified seamless recovery from process death at all IO stages.
- **Snapshot Fuzz Testing**: Proven deterministic rejection of corrupted state.
- **Rollback Replay Matrix**: Bit-for-bit equality check on cryptographically verified snapshots.

---

## Phase 13 — Multi-Novel Kernel & Cognitive Isolation (Completed)

**Goal:** Separate the engine from the book instance.
- **Project Layer**: introduced `ProjectPaths` and active project management (`--project-root`).
- **Novel DNA**: Schematized novel-specific voice, tone, and authorial influences.
- **Critic Constitution**: novel-specific editorial standards and hard failure rules.
- **Novel Bootstrapping**: `ingest` command for rapid project creation.

---

## Phase 14 — Prose Causality & Selection of Greatness (Completed)

**Goal:** Ground the system in technical mechanisms and elite human anchors.
- **3-Node Architecture**: Collapsed multi-agent loops to Generator -> Grounded Critic -> Tournament.
- **Corpus Oracle**: Multi-axis ground truth (Restraint vs. Formal Risk) anchored in Wolfe, Delany, Le Guin.
- **Causality Suite (MAT/MRT/MIT)**: Proven mechanism attribution via M1/M2/M3 baselines and 2x2 dependency matrices.
- **Adversarial Calibration v2**: Mechanism-based judging without adjective-based fluff.

---

## Phase 15 — The Adversarial Four Horsemen (Current)

**Goal:** Map the boundaries of judge intelligence and causal accuracy.
- **100-item Benchmark**: Pitting elite originals vs. fake greatness traps, ugly genius, and saturation failures.
- **Mechanism Isolation (MIT-2)**: Identifying necessity vs sufficiency of prose gears.
- **Mechanism Lawyer (Dialectic)**: Specialized adversarial agent for cross-examining causal claims.
- **Disagreement Gold**: Harvesting deltas between Human and Model technical rationales in the Disagreement Ledger.

---

## Phase 16 — Long-Horizon Preference Validation (Outstanding)

**Goal:** Solve for 'powerful' vs 'popular'.
- **Temporal Decay Test**: Separate scoring for Immediate Impact (T+0) vs. Meaningful Residue (T+7 days).
- **Mechanism Adversary Agent**: A dedicated agent whose only job is to attack and attempt to disprove identified mechanisms.
- **The Dangerous Genius Ledger**: Systematic analysis of 'ahead of taste' masterpieces rejected by human first-pass.

---

## Phase 17 — "Interview with the Devil" Template Reuse (Deferred)

**Goal:** Prove the architecture generalises to non-prose narrative tasks.

---

## Status Check
- **Generator**: Singular pass, high stakes.
- **Judge**: Adversarial, mechanism-based, corpus-anchored.
- **Selection**: Competitive selection for greatness, not compliance.
- **Causality**: Verified via counterfactual attribution.
